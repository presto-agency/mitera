What is the Mitera Agency Workbench?

Obtaining medical records can take over a month of outreach and paperwork. Scheduling screening services and waiting for replies can be overwhelming to manage with many hours of administrative time. The Mitera Agency Workbench simplifies your journey by providing everything you need, all in one place. It is an online platform that streamlines your screening process and offers medical records collection services, maternal-fetal medicine screening service bookings and psychological assessment options. 

How it works? 

Simply add a new client, either a gestational carrier candidate or intended parent. 
Book services and add-on services in the same timeframe, with no delays. 
Access, download or upload reports and records.
Register payment details for easy monthly invoicing.
Alerts are sent when reports are ready and appointments are coming up! 

Features 

Ease-of-Use: 
One-time case submission for medical records collections, Maternal-Fetal Medicine (MFM) screenings, psychological services and medical records collection. 
Accessibility:

Flexibility:
Add-on services customized to your needs.
