MFM Preconception Screening

Whether you are currently pregnant or planning pregnancy, a screening by an MFM subspecialist in addition to your standard care can optimize your pregnancy outcome and reduce risk for you and your baby. MFM subspecialists are the experts in the maternal and fetal complications of pregnancy.

MFM Gestational Carrier Screening

We provide comprehensive medical and obstetrical risk assessments of gestational carrier candidates by our MFM subspecialists. Involving an MFM early helps prevent adverse obstetrical outcomes as well provides as an opportunity for pregnancy optimization.

Reproductive Genetic Counseling

With the complexity of today’s screening and diagnostic modalities, having the opportunity to spend time with a certified reproductive genetic counselor is invaluable. We give you the opportunity to learn about all of the screening modalities and help you navigate the ever-evolving world of reproductive genetics.

About Mitera
Mitera gives you virtual access to maternal-fetal medicine (MFM) subspecialists, reproductive genetic counselors and other pregnancy experts and helps inform you about your genetic and other pregnancy-related risks through the latest available technologies, empowering you along your family-forming journey.
