Surrogacy Agencies

Mitera’s Maternal-Fetal Medicine doctors are ready to help you prepare for a pregnancy journey. Whether a mom-to-be or a gestational carrier, our board-certified physicians are ready to assess your pregnancy risk and provide their expert guidance for the best possible pregnancy outcomes.

Mitera has the only standardized screening and certification program for surrogacy agencies that providesboth initial obstetrical screening and medical oversight during the entire journey to mitigate risk and optimize pregnancy outcomes.

Fertility Centers

Let us take pregnancy risk assessment off your plate! Mitera’s team of board-certifiedMaternal-Fetal Medicine physicians (MFM’s)are licensed across the country and ready to help ease the load on fertilitycenters. Our MFM’s will manage the records review process in lieu of a nurse practitioner or reproductive endocrinologist and create a detailed report customized to the needs of the fertility practice. *

A Mitera certified program was specifically designed for surrogacy agencies and fertility centers. The program was created to engage and recognize leaders in 3rd party reproduction who are committed toupholding the highest medical standards. Surrogacy agencies and fertility centers that are certified by Mitera are recognized as being in the top tier in 3rd party reproduction providers.

Why Get Mitera Certified?

A Mitera Maternal-Fetal Medicine (MFM) clearance is highly regarded in 3rd party reproduction. A MFMclearance eases the concerns of a fertility physician and is often considered a “green light” for a surrogatecandidate to proceed with fertility treatment.

Suggested Appointment Types:

  • Ensures a thorough pregnancy risk assessment.
  • Mitigates risk and optimizes outcomes during pregnancy
  • Streamlines clinic pre-approval.
  • Prevents delays in a surrogacy journey.
  • Empowers both gestational carriers and intended parents.
  • Provide medicolegal protection for agencies.
Mitera Certification
Program

Surrogacy agencies and fertility centers that are certified have a formal relationship with Mitera that commits their organizations to:

  • Pre-match MFM screening of all surrogates

  • Compliance with our recommendations

  • Ongoing risk assessment and engagement during pregnancy

Our certified partners also have access to additional services such as MFM consultations for intended parents and at-home genetic testing and counseling
