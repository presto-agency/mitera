Insurance and payment policy

Please note that all Mitera appointments are considered fee-for-service and payment is due at the time of service. Although we do not file insurance claims, we provide you with the document called a ‘superbill’ that is necessary for you to file your claim with your insurance company and receive reimbursement, if you have applicable ‘out-of-network’ coverage. Please contact your insurance company directly to determine the level of reimbursement specific to your plan. We accept credit cards or debit and HSA/FSA cards with a major credit card logo. You will be asked to enter your payment information at the start of your appointment. The fee is dependent on the type of appointment, and you may see the fee by clicking on READ MORE for your appointment type in the below scheduler.

About Mitera
Mitera gives you virtual access to maternal-fetal medicine (MFM) subspecialists, reproductive genetic counselors and other pregnancy experts and helps inform you about your genetic and other pregnancy-related risks through the latest available technologies, empowering you along your family-forming journey.
