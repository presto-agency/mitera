Mitera Services

Mitera’s Maternal-Fetal Medicine doctors are ready to help you prepare for a pregnancy journey. Whether a mom-to-be or a gestational carrier, our board-certified physicians are ready to assess your pregnancy risk and provide their expert guidance for the best possible pregnancy outcomes.

Mitera offers subspecialty virtual appointments for moms-to-be, surrogates and intended parents working with a gestational carrier.Our modern approach to perinatal care offers flexibility, convenience and the expertise you deserve.*

Mitera offers the following virtual appointments:

  • Maternal-Fetal Medicine (MFM) Standard Preconception Screening
  • Maternal-Fetal Medicine (MFM) Gestational Carrier Screening
  • Maternal-Fetal Medicine (MFM) Intended Parent Follow up Consultation
  • Reproductive Genetic Counseling

Surrogacy Agencies, Gestational Carriers & Intended Parents

Mitera offers Gestational Carrier MFM Screening services to support surrogacy agencies, surrogates and intended parents which include a review of all available medical records by a Maternal-Fetal Medicine doctor (MFM), a virtual appointment with the surrogate candidate and a detailed customized report outlining risks and readiness for pregnancy.

Mitera has the only standardized screening and certification program for surrogacy agencies that provides both initial obstetrical screening and medical oversight during the entire journey to mitigate risks and optimize pregnancy outcomes.

Suggested Appointment Types:

  • Maternal-Fetal Medicine (MFM) Gestational Carrier Screening
  • Maternal-Fetal Medicine (MFM) Intended Parent Follow-Up Consultation
  • Reproductive Genetic Counseling

InPSYght Gestational Carrier Psychosocial Assessment Add-on Service

We provide comprehensive medical and obstetrical risk assessments of gestational carrier candidates by our MFM subspecialists. Involving an MFM early helps prevent adverse obstetrical outcomes as well provides as an opportunity for pregnancy optimization.

What’s Included:

  • Web-based, Tailored Gestational Carrier Psychosocial Evaluation: Gain insights into a surrogate’s mental health and readiness, identifying strengths and areas for improvement to boost their candidacy.
  • Early Insights, Less Commitment: Discover valuable information about a surrogate’s psychologicalreadiness without the initial commitment to a full evaluation, saving both time and resources.
  • Concurrent Screening Convenience: Our bundle allows for simultaneous medical and psychological assessments, streamlining the process and saving valuable time.
  • Special Rate for Full Evaluations: Should a full psychological evaluation be pursued post-screening, the candidate is eligible for a reduced rate and priority scheduling.
  • One-stop shop for medical screening and psychological pre-screen

*Mitera facilitators are not my facilitators of obstetric or genetic counseling care but rather facilitate an additional, independent third-party second opinion on risk stratification and/or assessment related to pregnancy (“Mitera Services”). No professional/patient relationship is created by using the information provided by or through the use of the Mitera Services.
