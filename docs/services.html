Surrogacy Agencies, Gestational Carriers & Intended Parents

Mitera offers Gestational Carrier MFM Screening services to support surrogacy agencies, surrogates and intended parents which include a review of all available medical records by a Maternal-Fetal Medicine doctor (MFM), a virtual appointment with the surrogate candidate and a detailed customized report outlining risks and readiness for pregnancy.

Mitera has the only standardized screening and certification program for surrogacy agencies that provides both initial obstetrical screening and medical oversight during the entire journey to mitigate risks and optimize pregnancy outcomes.

Suggested Appointment Types:

  • Maternal-Fetal Medicine (MFM) Gestational Carrier Screening
  • Maternal-Fetal Medicine (MFM) Intended Parent Follow-Up Consultation
