Technology-Empowered Pregnancy

Mitera offers a new obstetric care model that leverages maternal-fetal medicine (MFM) and reproductive genetics expertise through remote technologies to broaden access to advancements in genetic testing and fetal ultrasound.

Our unique virtual, at-home pregnancy genetic testing process empowers mothers and families anywhere with vital information about their reproductive journeys through patient education, test orders and kit processing and follow-up telehealth consultations, all while keeping their primary providers in the loop.

Largely due to a shortage of providers within maternal-fetal medicine (MFM) and reproductive genetics, millions of reproductive-aged women have limited access to the clinical expertise that can provide them with responsible guidance around available diagnostic technologies.

Through the use of various telehealth technologies and at-home testing kits, we not only extend access to that expertise, but we also make risk-appropriate obstetrics care the most convenient and patient-autonomous it's ever been.

About Mitera
